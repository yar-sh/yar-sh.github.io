The expansion resulted in an implosion, which produced unstable blobs of matter that will shape the new reality. The blobs seemingly have a possibility to continuously split themselves and the space around them, resulting in the 2^n expansion. To become stable, the blobs must all converge at a single point in space. And this convergence should happen not just at one level of expansion but on all of them - at the infinity depths.
